Apple growth stages · أطوار نمو التفاح

Fruit development · تطور الثمار
Based on Khenchela conditions (1,194m) · بناءً على ظروف خنشلة
Stage · الطورMonths · الأشهرDescription · الوصفKey actions · الأعمال الرئيسيةWatch for · انتبه لـ
Dormancy · السكون Dec – FebTree fully rested, no growth. Buds tight.Prune, apply dormant oil spray, soil test, prepare toolsDiseases entering wounds, rodent bark damage
Bud swell · انتفاخ البراعم Feb – MarBuds begin to swell and turn green. Vulnerable to frost.Apply nitrogen fertiliser, first fungicide spray, frost watch⚠️ Late frost at 1,194m — protect with covers if <0°C
Pink / Bloom · التزهير Mar – AprFlowers open. Critical pollination window.Support bees, scab prevention spray, no insecticides during bloomFrost damage to open flowers (most critical), fire blight
Petal fall · سقوط البتلات AprFlowers drop, small fruitlets visible.First insecticide (codling moth), copper spray, thin Block C flowersFruitlet drop, aphids starting
Fruit set · عقد الثمار Apr – MaySmall apple fruitlets developing fast.Fruit thinning Block B+F (6–8 per cluster → 1–2), drip irrigation, K+P startAphids, mites, June drop
Cell division · انقسام الخلايا May – JunCells multiplying — determines final fruit size.K+P fertiliser, irrigate every 5–7 days, spider mite monitoring🕷️ Spider mites in heat, lack of water = small fruit
▶ Fruit development · تطور الثمار
Current · الحالي 		Jun – AugFruit sizing rapidly. Peak water demand.Peak irrigation every 3–5 days, codling moth traps, stop N fertiliserCodling moth, sunburn on fruit, water stress
Colour / Maturity · النضج Aug – SepFruit changes colour and sweetens. Approach harvest.Reduce watering, starch-iodine test, pressure test, harvest trialOver-ripening, pre-harvest drop, wasps
Harvest · الحصاد 🌞 Aug–Sep / 🍂 Sep–Oct🌞 Blocks B, D, E (summer varieties) ready first. 🍂 Block F (autumn variety) follows.Starch-iodine test before picking, harvest early morning, grade A/B/rejected, record yield per blockBruising during picking, post-harvest rots, over-ripening
Post-harvest · ما بعد الحصاد Oct – NovTree recovers and stores energy for next year.Post-harvest fertiliser, copper spray, remove fallen fruit, mulch Block CCanker, collar rot, late season pests
